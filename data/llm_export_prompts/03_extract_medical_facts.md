Extract the key medical facts from the following report as a structured list.
Include: diagnoses, medications, procedures, dates of events, and any stated outcomes.
Do not infer or add information not present in the source text.
Keep anonymized placeholders (e.g. [PATIENT_NAME_001], [DATE_001]) unchanged.
Present each fact on its own line with a short category label.
